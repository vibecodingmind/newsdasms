    Visual messaging that captures hearts and minds

    MMS (Multimedia Messaging Service) lets your church go beyond text. Send vibrant event flyers, sermon preview images, worship audio clips, community project photos, and fundraising progress charts directly to your congregation's phones. With 15% higher click-through rates than plain SMS, MMS ensures your ministry messages don't just arrive — they make an impression. Our platform supports images, audio, video clips, vCards, QR codes, and interactive maps.

    Rich media features for your ministry

    Every media type your church needs to communicate visually and effectively.

    Image Messaging

    Send high-quality images directly to your congregation's phones. Share church event flyers, sermon graphics, and community photos with ease.

    Audio Messages

    Deliver audio clips of worship songs, sermon excerpts, and spoken blessings. Perfect for members who prefer listening over reading.

    Video Clips

    Share short video clips of worship sessions, testimonies, and ministry highlights. Engage your congregation with dynamic visual content.

    vCard Sharing

    Send digital contact cards for pastors, ministry leaders, and church staff. Members can save contact info directly to their phone with one tap.

    QR Code Delivery

    Embed QR codes in MMS messages for instant access to donation pages, event registrations, sermon links, and church websites.

    Map Locations

    Share interactive map locations for your church, outreach venues, and community events. Help visitors find you with built-in navigation.

    Slideshow MMS

    Create multi-slide MMS presentations that tell a story. Showcase ministry milestones, building projects, or mission trip highlights in sequence.

    Unicode Support

    Send MMS messages with Swahili, Arabic, and special characters. Communicate visually in any language your congregation speaks.

    Why visual messaging matters

    In a world of information overload, visual content cuts through the noise. MMS gives your ministry the power to communicate with impact.

    Visual Engagement

    Images and media capture attention far more effectively than text alone. Visual messages are processed 60,000x faster by the brain, making your church communications instantly impactful.

    Higher Conversion

    MMS messages with images see 15% higher click-through rates than plain SMS. When your congregation sees an event flyer, they're more likely to attend.

    Brand Recognition

    Consistent visual branding in every message reinforces your church identity. Your logo, colors, and design style build familiarity and trust.

    Stand Out in Inbox

    MMS messages are rare and attention-grabbing. While everyone sends text, a visual message from your church stands out and gets noticed.
